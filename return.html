Amazon ensures a seamless shopping experience with **Free International Returns** for eligible items.

How It Works

  • Return your item within **30 days** of delivery.
  • No extra charge for international return shipping.
  • Choose between a **full refund** or an exchange.
  • Easy return process directly from your Amazon account.

Return Process

  1. Go to **Your Orders** in your Amazon account.
  2. Select the item and click **Request a Return**.
  3. Print the prepaid return label provided.
  4. Pack and drop off your item at an authorized return center.
  5. Get your refund within **5-7 business days** after return processing.
